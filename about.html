About Make It By Hand with Terry Green

Allow our products to add the beauty of wood to your home.

What's The Purpose?

Make It By Hand is intended for people who enjoy having wooden products that enhance the beauty of their home. I especially enjoy building coffee/end tables and shadowboxes.

Who Am I?

My name is Terry Green, I'm a native of Mobile, AL but currently live in Crestview, FL. I've been working in the IT field for 20 years as a Progammer.

How did I get started?

I started creating wood products 30 years ago in middle school. Either it be wood or technology I've always enjoyed the feeling of accomplishment after building something.
